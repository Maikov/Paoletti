-----------------------------------------
OPENSHOP
-----------------------------------------
Version: Release [3th of November 2012]
Project site: http://OpenShop.org.ua

Mission: Make Opencart the best shop engine for beginners. 
Let it be usable without downloading and installing any additions.

Main features:
+ full admin area redesign & simplify
+ CMS functions
+ unused functionality hiding (like Shipping, VAT, Cart, Accounts & much more)
+ control any theme colors, backgrounds and animations
+ proper Digital Downloads with free product downloads
+ vQmod with manual mode to achieve top perfomance
+ ability to turn off OpenShop temporarily and come back to clean Opencart
+ multilanguage installer for new shops
